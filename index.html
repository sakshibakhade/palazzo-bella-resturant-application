About Palazzo Bella

Our story

Founded in 1990 by the De Luca family, Palazzo Bella has become a cherished destination for authentic Italian dining. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant blends traditional charm with a modern ambiance. With a deep respect for Italian culinary heritage, we use locally sourced produce and imported Italian ingredients to craft every dish with care.

Our menu features beloved classics like wood-fired pizza, handmade pasta, and artisan desserts, all made fresh daily.Our service is to good then other resturant.
